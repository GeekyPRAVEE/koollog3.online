About Koollog

Temperature measurement is important wherever temperature-sensitive products are studied, produced, stored or transported. Koollog offers a range of temperature measuring instruments to this end: from temperature probes and transmitters to handhelds and data loggers. Koollog Monitoring System makes it possible to access temperature and other necessary measured data at any time from anywhere in the world.KOOLLOG, the most advanced Temperature Monitoring system in India. It is a Digital Sensor based real-time, truly online system, designed Hashprompt Technologies us and manufactured in INDIA with International Standards. We have over 12 years of experience in providing Temperature Monitoring Services in India and in the United States.Koollog is a cloud-based remote monitoring system that monitors your most important environmental variables including temperature and humidity. Collect all your data in a single platform, and monitor and receive alerts on any device.
